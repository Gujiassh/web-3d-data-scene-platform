# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standards

Project spaces must remain professional, respectful and useful. Examples of expected behavior include constructive review,
clear technical disagreement, respect for different backgrounds and experience levels, and responsibility for correcting
mistakes.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing, sustained disruption and publishing another
person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may warn, temporarily restrict or permanently ban participants whose
behavior is inappropriate, threatening or harmful. Enforcement decisions should consider context, impact, repetition and
the safety of affected participants.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through GitHub. Reports will be handled with discretion;
retaliation against a reporter or participant in an investigation is prohibited.

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews and project-related public events.
